About the Project

This project aims to provide a simple and effective way to assess the quality of water using artificial intelligence (AI). The goal is to create a tool that can quickly analyze water samples by either webcam or image upload, helping to determine whether the water is clean or contaminated.

The initiative is aligned with the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal (SDG) 6, which aims to "ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all." By leveraging AI technology, we strive to contribute to better water quality monitoring, increasing awareness about the importance of clean water for communities worldwide.

You can upload images or open the camera to analyze the water sample.
